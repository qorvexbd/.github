## 🏢 About Qorvex Technology BD

**Qorvex Technology BD** is a Bangladeshi deep-tech startup specializing in **IoT, Robotics, and Embedded Systems** — founded in 2026 and based in Rangpur, Bangladesh.

We design and manufacture smart devices built from the ground up for real-world use: privacy-first, locally engineered, and delivered nationwide across all 64 districts.

This GitHub organization hosts our **open-source firmware, utilities, and tools** — everything battle-tested on real Qorvex hardware, published for makers, students, and engineers across Bangladesh and beyond.

## 🌟 Why Open Source?

> *"Innovation doesn't have to come from a foreign lab or cost a foreign price."*

We open-source our work because the Bangladeshi maker community deserves **real, production-grade reference material** — not just beginner tutorials.

If you're a student at a polytechnic, a maker tinkering with ESP32s, or a professional engineer in Bangladesh — **this is built for you.**

### What you'll find here:
- ✅ Production firmware used in real Qorvex products
- ✅ Reusable drivers, libraries, and utilities
- ✅ Documented, readable, and community-maintained code
- ✅ Designed for hardware available in Bangladesh
